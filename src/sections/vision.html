Our Core Principles

Inspired by Nature

We believe nature holds the most efficient algorithms. Our work is a journey from the tangible world of robotics to the abstract beauty of mathematics, grounded in a sense of wonder for the grand design of creation.

Purpose-Built Innovation

We build proprietary software and purpose-built robotics that master the unstructured complexity of the real world. Our designs ensure our solutions are rugged, low-cost, and perfectly suited for the environments they operate in.

Driving Collective Growth

We believe in harnessing technology for collective growth. Our goal is to boost resource productivity, reduce environmental impact, and drive economic prosperity across critical sectors. We measure success by the value we create—from enabling sustainable practices on Earth to opening new frontiers in space.

Resilience and Security

Our systems are designed for resilience, with hybrid power and a security model emphasizing fail-safes and remote overrides. We build robust platforms that operators can trust in the most demanding conditions.
