Our First Mission: An Intelligent Fleet for Precision Agriculture

We deploy a fleet of intelligent, ground-based robotic arms designed for the complexities of modern agriculture. Our platform uses dynamic, AI-driven routing to ensure each unit of work is completed with maximum efficiency. These robots work collectively to get the job done quickly and precisely, transforming how critical farm tasks are performed.

Priority Use Cases: High-Value, Low-Saturation Niches

We are targeting key agricultural niches where our technology provides a clear and immediate advantage over manual labor and existing machinery.

Precision Harvesting of Specialty Crops

Problem: Manual harvesting of delicate, high-value crops is labor-intensive and prone to damage, impacting farmer income.

6DOF Advantage: Our robot can selectively pick ripe produce, identify ripeness with computer vision, and use gentle grasping or cutting tools to harvest without causing damage.

Intelligent Weeding and Targeted Spraying

Problem: Manual weeding is backbreaking, and blanket spraying wastes chemicals and harms the environment. Drones lack ground-level precision.

6DOF Advantage: Our ground-based system uses computer vision to precisely detect weeds at the individual plant level and apply a micro-dose of herbicide, or perform mechanical weeding, ensuring minimal chemical usage and greater environmental safety.

Nursery and Seed Tray Management

Problem: The repetitive, delicate task of seedling pick-and-place is a major bottleneck in commercial nurseries.

6DOF Advantage: The arm's gentle, consistent handling ensures higher throughput and reduces seedling damage, freeing workers for higher-value tasks.

Post-Harvest Sorting and Grading

Problem: Manual sorting of produce is slow and inconsistent, leading to lower quality grades and reduced market value.

6DOF Advantage: A multi-arm, vision-based system can sort and grade by size, color, ripeness, and quality with unparalleled efficiency and consistency.
