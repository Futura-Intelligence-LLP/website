Our Strategic Roadmap

Our journey is structured in three core phases, designed to de-risk our technology, establish a scalable business model, and drive long-term growth.

Phase 1: Emergence & Validation

(First 18 Months)

Objective: Develop our Minimum Viable Product (MVP), secure pilot partners, and validate our technology and business model in real-world agricultural settings.

Key Deliverables:

  • Develop and test the MVP for a priority use case (e.g., grape harvesting).
  • Conduct field validation with a State Agricultural University or ICAR institution to prove reliability.
  • Deploy via Custom Hiring Centers (CHCs) to establish the RaaS model and document ROI.

Phase 2: Commercial Scaling & Expansion

(Future)

Objective: Scale our Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS) model through the CHC network and expand our hardware offerings for new high-value applications.

Key Deliverables:

  • Expand the CHC partner network to increase market penetration with small and marginal farmers.
  • Develop specialized end-effectors for new use cases like vineyard pruning and greenhouse automation.
  • Grow our engineering team to enhance the platform and integrate predictive maintenance.

Phase 3: Ubiquitous Intelligence

(Long-Term Vision)

Objective: Apply our core intelligent manipulation platform to solve complex challenges in new, unstructured environments beyond agriculture.

Key Deliverables:

  • Identify and enter new markets where dexterous robotics is a bottleneck (e.g., urban maintenance, logistics, waste sorting).
  • Develop next-generation, self-learning systems that adapt to new tasks and environments.
  • Achieve a scale that delivers measurable impact on Earth and builds the foundational technology for off-world applications.
