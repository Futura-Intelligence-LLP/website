---
title: "Our Strategic Roadmap"
intro: "Our journey is structured in three core phases, designed to de-risk our technology, establish a scalable business model, and drive long-term growth."
phases:
  - title: "Phase 1: Emergence & Validation"
    duration: "(Project Quarters 1-6)"
    objective: "Develop our Minimum Viable Product (MVP), secure pilot partners, and validate our core technology and business model in real-world settings."
    milestones:
      - "Complete foundational R&D, including mathematical modeling and simulation for autonomous navigation and manipulator control."
      - "Fabricate and test the physical MVP: purpose-built, low-cost robotic arms and mobility mechanisms, fully integrated with our core software and hybrid power system."
      - "Secure field validation partners (e.g., a State Agricultural University or ICAR institution) to benchmark the MVP's performance, reliability, and security."
      - "Deploy via Custom Hiring Centers (CHCs) in a pilot program to establish the RaaS model and document end-user ROI."

  - title: "Phase 2: Commercial Scaling & Expansion"
    duration: "(Project Quarters 7-8)"
    objective: "Scale our Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS) model through the CHC network and expand our hardware offerings for new high-value applications."
    milestones:
      - "Expand the CHC partner network to increase market penetration with small and marginal farmers."
      - "Develop specialized end-effectors and custom arms for new use cases (e.g., vineyard pruning, greenhouse automation)."
      - "Grow our engineering team to enhance the platform and integrate predictive maintenance."

  - title: "Phase 3: Ubiquitous Intelligence"
    duration: "(Year 3+)"
    objective: "Apply our core intelligent manipulation platform to solve complex challenges in new, unstructured environments beyond agriculture."
    milestones:
      - "Identify and enter new markets where dexterous robotics is a bottleneck (e.g., urban maintenance, logistics, waste sorting)."
      - "Develop next-generation, self-learning systems that adapt to new tasks and environments."
      - "Achieve a scale that delivers measurable impact on Earth and builds the foundational technology for off-world applications."
---
